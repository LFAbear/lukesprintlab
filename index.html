The Maker Behind the Magic

Meet Luke

Hi, I'm Luke — I'm 8 years old, in second grade, and the youngest of four kids. I started 3D printing about 18 months ago on an X-Maker, and I've leveled up since then — I now run a Bambu Labs X1C and an H2C Laser Edition. I love both printing and designing my own projects from scratch.

Check out my gallery — I've printed water guns, fidget toys, RC cars, candy vending machines, safes, coin sorting vaults, and even shoes. I can also design logos, print coasters, or make custom business cards.

One day I hope to open my own full Print Lab with my Dad. Until then, if you have an idea or a custom project in mind — reach out and let's make it happen!
